New paper on solving robust PCA and matrix completion by non-convex gradient descent.

About

I am a 4th year PhD student in Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at The University of Texas at Austin. I am advised by Constantine Caramanis. I obtained my B.E. from Tsinghua University in 2012. I have spent two summers as an intern at Google, where I made contributions to Google's recommendation and ads systems.

My research interests include machine learning, high-dimensional statistics, convex and non-convex optimization. I particularly enjoy developing tractable non-convex algorithms for large scale learning problems.
